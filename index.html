Synopsis

A lone woman moves through a dead world. Temple spires and collapsed buildings drift under a pale grey sky. She carries one weapon. She asks no questions. Something finds her.

The world she walks through has no name she knows of. It does not age. It does not heal. The ruins of a civilization long fractured hang suspended in the air around her — caught between collapse and something that never came after.

She has no memory of before. She has a mission. That is enough. For now.

Statement

Generative AI video has made remarkable progress in a short amount of time. What once required entire production pipelines can now be explored by a single person with the right tools. The limitations are still real — consistency, physics, fine control — but the gap between idea and execution has never been smaller.

This project is an attempt to push that boundary — specifically around action sequences and micro storytelling. With Omni-Reference now available across most leading video models, character consistency across shots has become genuinely achievable. This pilot tests exactly that: how far the technology holds when the story demands movement, tension, and continuity.
